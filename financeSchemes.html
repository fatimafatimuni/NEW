Our Finance Scheme

HBL

At Auto World, we are dedicated to making your dream of owning a quality vehicle a reality. With our flexible and customer-friendly finance scheme, you can enjoy the freedom of choosing the perfect car while benefiting from attractive terms and competitive rates. We offer financing options of up to 15 lac's, with interest rates as low as 5% APR. Our financing terms range from 8 months to 24 months, giving you the flexibility to select a repayment plan that suits your budget. Whether you're looking for a shorter repayment period with higher monthly installments or an extended term with lower monthly payments, we have you covered.
